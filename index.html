Paraíba, Brazil

Paraíba is a state in northeastern Brazil known for its beautiful Atlantic coastline, rich cultural traditions, and historic cities. Its capital, João Pessoa, is one of the oldest cities in Brazil and is famous for its beaches, colonial architecture, and vibrant local culture. The state's economy is supported by agriculture, tourism, and commerce, while its warm climate and welcoming people make it a popular destination for visitors.
